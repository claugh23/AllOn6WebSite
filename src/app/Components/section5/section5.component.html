We are a clinic located in Costa Rica, with more than 14 years of experience in dental implants and specialists in the All on 6 technique. This technique consists of placing 6 implants, where a titanium porcelain bridge is placed that is fixed to these implants, in this way, in a single trip to Costa Rica and in a few days, we can give our patients a completely new smile thanks to our internal laboratory and our highly trained specialists. The all on six technique is a technique that is used in the rehabilitation of the upper and lower jaw when a serious resorption of the bone has not yet been detected, schedule your appointment and transform your life with a new smile.
